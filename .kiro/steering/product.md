# Product Overview

Professional hair salon website template designed for modern salons to establish their online presence.

## Core Features

- Service catalog (haircuts, coloring, treatments, special occasions)
- Blog system for salon news and tips
- Contact form for customer inquiries
- Team member profiles
- Gallery showcase
- Booking integration ready
- Analytics and consent management
- SEO optimized with sitemap and metadata

## Target Users

Hair salons, beauty professionals, and stylists looking for a production-ready website template with modern design and functionality.

## Key Characteristics

- Mobile-first responsive design
- Accessibility compliant
- Performance optimized
- SEO friendly
- Privacy-focused (GDPR ready)
