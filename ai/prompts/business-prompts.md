# Business Prompts

This file contains curated prompts for AI agents to assist with business analysis and decision-making for the hair salon application.

## Business Analysis Prompts

### Market Analysis
```
You are a business analyst specializing in the salon and beauty industry. Analyze the market opportunity for a hair salon business:

Market Analysis Scope:
- Geographic area: [city/region]
- Target demographic: [age group, income level, etc.]
- Service focus: [hair services, beauty treatments, etc.]
- Competitive landscape: [number of competitors, market saturation]

Analysis Requirements:
- Market size and growth potential
- Competitive analysis and positioning
- Customer behavior and preferences
- Pricing trends and elasticity
- Regulatory and compliance considerations
- Technology adoption in the industry
- Seasonal patterns and trends

Please provide:
1. Market size assessment with growth projections
2. Competitive landscape analysis with SWOT
3. Customer segmentation and persona development
4. Pricing strategy recommendations
5. Market positioning and differentiation strategy
6. Risk assessment and mitigation strategies

Analysis Framework:
- Use industry reports and market research
- Consider local economic factors
- Analyze demographic trends and preferences
- Evaluate technology adoption patterns
- Consider regulatory and licensing requirements
- Assess seasonal and cyclical factors

Focus on actionable insights that drive business strategy and investment decisions.
```
