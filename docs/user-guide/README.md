## Booking Appointments

### Step-by-Step Booking Process

1. **Navigate to Booking**: Click "Book Appointment" in the navigation
2. **Select Service**: Choose from our available services
3. **Choose Date & Time**: Select your preferred appointment time
4. **Provide Information**: Enter your contact details
5. **Confirm Booking**: Review and confirm your appointment

### Available Services

- **Haircuts**: Classic cuts, modern styles, children's cuts
- **Coloring**: Full color, highlights, balayage, ombre
- **Treatments**: Deep conditioning, keratin treatments
- **Styling**: Special event styling, updos
- **Consultations**: Hair analysis and recommendations

### Booking Policies

- **Cancellation**: Please cancel at least 24 hours in advance
- **Late Arrival**: Arrivals more than 15 minutes late may require rescheduling
- **Payment**: We accept cash, credit cards, and digital payments

### Appointment Reminders

You'll receive:

- Email confirmation immediately after booking
- Reminder email 24 hours before your appointment
- SMS reminder (if phone number provided)

## FAQ

### Booking Questions

**Q: How far in advance should I book?**
A: We recommend booking 1-2 weeks in advance for popular times. Weekend appointments fill up quickly.

**Q: Can I book the same day?**
A: Same-day appointments are sometimes available for weekdays. Call us for availability.

**Q: What if I need to cancel?**
A: Cancel at least 24 hours in advance to avoid cancellation fees.

### Service Questions

**Q: How long does a typical appointment take?**
A: Haircuts usually take 45-60 minutes. Color services can take 2-4 hours depending on the service.

**Q: Do you offer consultations?**
A: Yes, we offer free 15-minute consultations for new clients.

**Q: What products do you use?**
A: We use professional-grade products from top brands. All products are available for purchase.

### Payment Questions

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept cash, all major credit cards, and digital payments like Apple Pay and Google Pay.

**Q: Do you require a deposit?**
A: Deposits are required for appointments over 2 hours and for new clients during peak times.

**Q: Can I tip on credit card?**
A: Yes, tips can be added to credit card payments or given in cash.

### Technical Support

**Q: I'm having trouble with the online booking system.**
A: Try refreshing the page or using a different browser. If issues persist, call us directly.

**Q: I didn't receive my booking confirmation email.**
A: Check your spam folder first. If still not found, contact us to confirm your appointment.

**Q: Can I book multiple services at once?**
A: Yes, you can book multiple services in a single appointment. Our system will calculate the appropriate time needed.
