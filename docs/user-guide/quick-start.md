# Quick Start Guide

Get up and running with the Hair Salon application in minutes.

## For Customers

### Book Your First Appointment

1. **Visit our website**: Open your browser and go to our salon website
2. **Click "Book Appointment"**: Find the booking button in the navigation menu
3. **Choose your service**: Select from our list of available services
4. **Pick a time**: Choose your preferred date and time from available slots
5. **Enter your details**: Fill in your name, email, and phone number
6. **Confirm booking**: Review your appointment details and confirm

That's it! You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details.
